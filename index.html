A laconic eyepatch-wearing teenage girl, a representative from the land of magnets and pudding, a paranoid nihilist, a wish-granting doll of dubious origin, and a shut-in we honestly don't know much about have, in the least likely of alliances, teamed up to form Pain-Feeled Substation, a neo-geocache of speculative writing for your ravenous eye-brains. We've done all the paperwork, and you're allowed to look inside our heads, so, welcome!

H. K. Oubliette analyzes memories of a childhood favorite game, but not hers, as well as five poems she wants you to stick on the fridge.

Yvette Wheeler wants you to meet Needle, whose worldview is just as enchanting as she is miserable, in the poem collection "Three for Needle."

Tomac Gau delights us with an exciting new serial about the psychic travails of losers and burnouts in necrosuburbia in the first episode of Rockstar Kids and provides us with yet another creation myth in "Entered the World."

Anomalous Love Doll has traveled a long way to meet you. Please treat her gently in "Nascent Cundite," the incipient chapter of the serial PUELLA AETERNA.

Yuri Fufurin dredges up the memorial ruins of a long-sleeping world in his poem, "Embryo."
